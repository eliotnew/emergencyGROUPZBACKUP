Before the next session, we'd like you to carry out a very short activity:


1. Think of 1 thing that you usually think if someone stares at you, turns away from you or ignores you.

2. Think of 2 things that you might say if people ask you about your appearance.

3. Think of 3 things that you could do if people stare.
